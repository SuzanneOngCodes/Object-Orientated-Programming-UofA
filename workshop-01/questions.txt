What is the purpose of the log message you supply to your svn commit?
To identify the changes made from the user in the files or directories that are committed

What do you think makes a good commit message?
It should be concise and informative of what changes have been made in this commit

When and how often should I be committing my work?
Once any changes are made in the files or directories to update the master copy in the version control servers

True or False, you should commit all files (including binaries, images, and log files) to SVN.
TRUE, because it sends and update changes of all the files and directories to the version control servers
